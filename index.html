About RandomDailyPhotos™ 


RandomDailyPhotos™ is a photo discovery app that presents to the user daily 'photo of the day' from bing. it also allows users to share photos with friends.

Features
  • Presents Photos of Day & Metadata about each Photo.
  • Get Photos accordingly to your local language culture e.g en-US.
  • Get Upto 16 past Photos of day from current date
  • Share Photos with Family & Friends.

How to Use
  • Click on 'RandomDailyPhotos' Title on the toolbar to refresh photos and get more previous photos of the day(upto 16).
  • Go to about page(info icon) to set the language culture toggle button accordingly to get local photos
  • Click on the 'Share' icon on the toolbar to share photos with family & friends. Enjoy!
